ABOUT DEPARTMENT

The Department of Basic Sciences is established in the year 1997. The Department consists of well qualified and experienced faculty members. The Department aims at providing outstanding teaching and quality training in science to all students at all levels in all disciplines. The department has two Ph.D. holders and four faculties are pursuing their Ph.D. The Department of Physics conducts courses in Engineering Physics, Laser Physics and Non-linear Optics, Advanced Physics for Engineers. It has a recognized Research Center under VTU. The Department of Chemistry conducts courses in Engineering Chemistry, Science of Nano Materials -Synthesis & Characterization Techniques. The Department of Mathematics conducts courses in Engineering Mathematics, Advanced Mathematics, Applied Mathematics, Additional Mathematics, Discrete Mathematical Structures, Operations Research, Graph Theory and Combinatorics, Probability and Statistics, Linear Algebra, Numerical Methods and Applications, Statistics and Numerical Methods. The Department also identifies students who need extra mentoring in the subjects and conducts remedial classes in order to enhance the performance of the students in VTU examination. The Physics Department also has a Research Center recognized by VTU.

VISION

“To impart value-based quality technical education in Electronics and Communication Engineering by empowering the students with research and teamwork capabilities and making them socially and ethically strong”

MISSION

M1:To provide superior learning experience with state of art laboratories, competent staff and industry driven skill development thereby improving employability with agility
M2: To keep abreast with latest technological advancements through innovations and research work, nurture entrepreneurial and leadership skills
M3: To inculcate professional ethics and responsible engineering practices to meet societal needs

Our Infrastructure

The Department of Physics has built-up area of 207 square meters, which includes laboratory, staff room, store room and Research center. The laboratory is equipped with modern instruments for conduction of advanced experiments. The Department of Chemistry is housed in 200 square meters, which includes laboratory, staff room and store rooms. The laboratory is equipped with sophisticated instruments and apparatus.
