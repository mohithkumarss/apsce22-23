Admission Process at APSCE

 

Admission to First Year B.E. course in APSCE is through three modes viz. KCET, COMEDK & Management Quota. The Lateral entry admissions of Diploma students for second year B.E. courses is only through Diploma KCET. Institution's Administrative Office staff provides assistance to young talented engineering seat aspirants in the admission process by guiding them through the process until they get settled in their respective courses.
