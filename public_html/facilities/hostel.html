Student Hostels
The campus has a Hostel for men, which is a home away from home for nearly 150 students hailing from different states all over the country. Adequate leisure is ensured to the hostilities by way of General Library, Entertainment Centre, Indoor and Outdoor Sports facility and even a gymnasium. Well furnished rooms with an accommodation choice of single, double and triple occupancy are available. In other to go with this industrial trends and standard, a student needs to learn some extra technical skills which are more to practical oriented. So we provide our students the opportunities to learn extra industrial technical skills which provide them the confidence to compete with other students in the industry.
