Alumni Meet - up

NOSTALGIA 2021

ALUMNI MEET (NOSTALGIA 2021) was held on 18th September 2021. The program was conducted to gather passed out students of our college from 2001 onwards. Around 100 ALUMNIs participated in the meet.

Program was started by the invocation by Anagha, 7th Sem CSE student. The function was graced by Prof K P Narasimha Murthy, Vice President, APSET and GC Chairman, APSCE and Sri A R Acharya, General Secretary, APSET. Function was formally inaugurated by lighting the lamp by the Management Representatives, ALUMNIs and Principal Prof Jagadeesh H S. Prof C P Sameerana, Faculty, Dept of CSE (2002 passed out - CSE) anchored the event.

To start with Prof Jagadeesh H S, Principal welcomed the gathering and shared his thoughts about the way the ALUMNIs can involve with the college. This was followed by the address by the Management Representatives who urged the ALUMNIs to maintain close contact with the college and contribute back to the Institution which shaped their careers. Then, Prof Ravi Kumar, TPO made a detailed presentation on present placement opportunities given to the students and the ways in which ALUMNI can strengthen the placement department. This was followed by ideas and thought sharing by the ALUMNIs. Around 15 gave their valuable thoughts and suggestions for the betterment of the college in all the aspects. They also assured in assisting placement, projects and other technical related aspects. All the ALUMNIs requested the college to organize such functions regularly.
