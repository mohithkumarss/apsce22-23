Principal' s Message

Hearty Welcome to Green Campus. Change is the only constant that is happening in the world. World is changing fast due to fast advancement of technology as such we should be on our toes for the change expected in these modern days. Government of India at the center and Government of Karnataka are adopting latest curriculum in engineering colleges. National Education Policy-2020(NEP) is one such program happening to bring lot of changes in the field education in general and engineering in particular. Our Institution is ready for the change that is happening in state and national level to give our students the best through technical, non technical, general knowledge and ethical to make future responsible citizens of country. The young minds are nurtured towards competing world class changes. With the vision of reaching higher education through professional curriculum to the needy minds to succeed their life with greater ambitions. The dedicated staff will inspire towards learning from basic concepts to industry requirements. The Industry and Institute interaction will provide couple of opportunities to adapt the upcoming technologies with agility. Complete your journey of four years with passion to reach destiny. The human life is a journey with holistic development such as ethical, cultural, Sports along with Academics. Once again welcome to the peace environment towards building better future.

Dr. A.G. Nataraj

B.E, M.Tech, Ph.D
