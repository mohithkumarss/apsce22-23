ABOUT DEPARTMENT

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering was established in the year 1997, currently running with student intake of 60 for Undergraduate program under Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi. The department is imparting quality technical education to students. The department strives not only for the academic excellence but also lays emphasis on holistic development of the students through highly caliber, qualified and dedicated faculty members. The students are given challenging academic environment, well equipped laboratories, well stocked departmental library and computer networking facilities. In addition to the prescribed curriculum, students are encouraged to take on Internship and Project work to gain hands on experience. Regular interaction with experts from various institutes and research centers expand the frontiers of knowledge of the students. Industrial visits are arranged as part of the curriculum. Conferences, Student seminars and workshops contribute to enhance the effectiveness of learning. Alumni interaction is a regular activity associated in line with academics.

VISION

“To impart value-based quality technical education in Electronics and Communication Engineering by empowering the students with research and teamwork capabilities and making them socially and ethically strong”

MISSION

M1:To provide superior learning experience with state of art laboratories, competent staff and industry driven skill development thereby improving employability with agility
M2: To keep abreast with latest technological advancements through innovations and research work, nurture entrepreneurial and leadership skills
M3: To inculcate professional ethics and responsible engineering practices to meet societal needs

Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

At the end of the program, the graduate will be able to :
    PSO 1: Understand the concepts of Electronics & Communication engineering and their applications in the field of semiconductor technology, consumer electronics, embedded system, communication/ networking and other relevant areas.
    PSO 2: Apply technical knowledge and usage of modern hardware & software tools related to Electronics & Communication engineering for solving real world problems.
    PSO 3: Analyze, comprehend, design & develop electronic subsystems/ systems for a variety of engineering applications and thus demonstrate professional ethics & concern for societal well being.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

The Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) specialized in Electronics and Communication Engineering is designed to achieve the following objectives after four to five years of graduation. They will be able to :
    PEO 1: Recognize, synthesize and apply the acquired knowledge in basic sciences and mathematics in solving Electronics and Communication Engineering problems.
    PEO 2: Design and implement analog and digital electronic circuits for modern communication systems with given specifications.
    PEO 3: Communicate effectively, inculcate the habit of team work, demonstrate leadership qualities and exhibit professional conduct in their career.
    PEO 4: Involve in life-long self-learning, career enhancement and adapt to changing multi disciplinary professional and societal needs.

Our Infrastructure

The department is located in an area of 8000 sq.ft. The labs are well equipped with about 50 computer units of high performance. It has Lecture halls, Departmental Library, Faculty room, and Laboratories. The major Laboratories are:
  1. Analog Electronics Circuits Lab
  2. Power Electronics Lab
  3. Analog Communication & Lic Lab
  4. VLSI Lab/HDL Lab
  5. DSP Lab / Microcontroller Lab / mp Lab
  6. Advanced Communication Lab
  7. Logic Design Lab

Faciliities in ECE Department

ECE department is located in an area of 8000 sq.ft. The Department has laboratories such as Analog and Digital Electronics, Digital Signal Processing, VHDL / Verilog, Microcontroller, Communication Lab, Computer Communication Networks, VLSI and other basic laboratories required for conducting experiments pertaining to VTU syllabus. FAQ

  • Why should one opt for Electronics & Communication Engineering?

    Answer. ECE-Electronics & Communication Engineering is a course of applying scientific principles to the design and development, fabrication, repair & maintenance of Electrical/Electronic Machines, Instruments and Communication systems. It is a core subject which has enormous job opportunities in either hardware or software field. It covers a wide range of day today applications like Television, Radio, Computers, and Mobile phones etc. They help us to see, hear and communicate over vast distances and do things faster. No one could imagine that telephone, invented by Alexander Graham Bell, the radio, developed by Marconi, two distinct technologies come together would create a wonderful product that is Cell Phone (smart phones) which is now part of everybody’s life. Knowledge of Electronics is required in all domains of engineering like Chemical, Aeronautical, Electrical, Mechanical, Computer, Civil, Automobile sectors etc.

  • What are the career opportunities available to Electronics and Communication Engineers?

    Answer. ECE students can develop an exciting career in industries as they are employed in variety of sectors such as Software Engineering/IT, Consumer Electronics manufacturing organization, Health care equipment manufacturing, Mobile communication (2G,3G,4G), Internet technologies, Telecommunication, , Power sector, Hardware Manufacturing, Home Appliance and VLSI design, Television Industry and Research & Development. Graduates in Electronics Engineering also have lots of opportunities in Government and private companies in the areas of design, manufacture, installation, operation, and maintenance of electronics equipment and systems. Electronics & Communication Engineering offers career scope in DRDO (Defense Research and Development Organization), ISRO (Indian Space Research Organization), Public and Private Sectors, Defense Services and Engineering services. Indian Telephone Industries, Civil Aviation, Development Centers in various States. Some of the job titles include, Service Engineer, Software Analyst, Technical Director, Field Test Engineer, Senior Sales Manager, Network Planning Engineer, Customer Support Engineer, Electronics and Communications Consultant, Research & Development Software Engineer. Electronics and Communication Engineers get equal opportunity to explore and build a career in IT sectors.

  • What is the scope of an Electronic and Communication engineer in the current trend of “Make in India” concept.

    Answer. ‘Make in India’ concept is a push to manufacturing sector and will open plethora of jobs for Electronics Engineer. Set up of 8 electronics manufacturing hubs and 7 electronics manufacturing clusters across the country to reduce imports. PMs ‘Digital India’ vision: 2,50,000 villages to be integrated with National Optical Fibre Network. National Policy on Electronics: To make India a globally competitive destination for Electronics System Design and Manufacturing by 2020.

  • What are the salary prospects?

    Answer. The job market for electronics and communication engineering is on demand and industries are looking for qualified engineers who can assist them on latest technologies. With recent technological advancements, the scope for the electronics engineers is on the rise. However, according to payscale.com the average annual salary of these professional includes Rs. 312,408 per year. Electronics students with skills in robotics, automation technologies, renewable energies, Internet of Things (IoT), mechatronics engineering concepts add more value to their average pay scale.
